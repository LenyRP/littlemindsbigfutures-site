Why College Grove families choose Leslie

A tutor who won't mind the drive

I'm Leslie Dykstra, a certified early childhood specialist with 10+ years working with young learners across Williamson County. College Grove's country roads and horse farms feel a world away from the busier parts of the county, and I love that about it — it's a genuinely peaceful place to raise a child.

College Grove is a bit further out than some of the areas I serve, but it's still a manageable drive from our Arrington studio, and I'm glad to come to you for in-home sessions too. Many of the families I work with here are homeschooling or blending homeschool with co-op days, and one-on-one tutoring fits naturally into that rhythm — no classroom pacing, no waiting for the rest of the group.

Whether your child is just starting to sound out letters or working through a stubborn reading gap, we start with where they actually are and build from there — patiently, and with a plan you can see working.

Good to know

Questions College Grove families ask

Anything else on your mind? Just ask Leslie.

Do you tutor families in College Grove?+

Yes. College Grove is a short drive from our Arrington studio, and I regularly come to College Grove families for in-home sessions as well. Online sessions are also available for families who prefer them.

Is tutoring available for children who aren't in a traditional school yet?+

Absolutely. Many College Grove families I work with are homeschooling or considering it. Sessions are one-on-one and built around your child, whether they attend a classroom, are homeschooled, or haven't started school at all yet.

What does a first session look like?+

We start with a free 15-minute conversation about your child, then an assessment to understand exactly where they are. From there, we build a plan tailored to their age, personality, and goals.
