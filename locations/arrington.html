Why Arrington families choose Leslie

This is where it all started

I'm Leslie Dykstra, a certified early childhood specialist, and Arrington isn't just a service area for me — it's home. Little Minds, Big Futures began right here, born out of years working with young learners and a conviction that every child deserves a tutor who actually knows them, not just their reading level.

Because I'm local, Arrington families get the shortest drive to studio sessions and the easiest scheduling for in-home visits. Whether we meet near the Arrington Vineyards area or closer to the Highway 840 corridor, it's a quick trip either way. Families out toward Franklin, Nolensville, and College Grove are close by too, so a lot of the children I tutor grow up knowing each other from church, sports, or school.

Whether your child needs a gentle nudge toward kindergarten readiness or focused help closing a reading gap, we start with a real conversation about your child — not a generic intake form. Sessions are one-on-one, faith-friendly, and built around your family's actual life.
