Why Brentwood families choose Leslie

Certified, experienced, and genuinely invested

With 10+ years in early childhood education and hundreds of children walked alongside, I bring Goddard-level curriculum expertise to a personal, 1-on-1 setting. Every session is tailored to your child — not a generic program designed for a classroom of 20.

Most of the young learners I meet from Brentwood attend Williamson County schools like Kenrose, Edmondson, or Crockett elementaries, where expectations are high and the classroom pace is quick. That's a good problem to have — but it also means a child who's even slightly behind in phonics or number sense can start to feel it early. One-on-one sessions give your child room to catch up, and then get ahead, without the pressure of keeping up with nineteen classmates.

Brentwood is also one of the easiest places for me to reach. From Arrington, it's a straight shot up Wilson Pike, and the neighborhoods off Split Log Road and Concord Road are practically next door. In-home sessions usually happen right at your kitchen table — after school, before dinner, whenever fits — and I bring everything your child needs.

I serve Brentwood families with flexible session options. I'm happy to come to you, meet at our studio in Arrington, or work with your child online. Whatever makes this sustainable for your family is what we do.
