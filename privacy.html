Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026 · Little Minds Big Futures LLC (d/b/a Little Minds, Big Futures)

Little Minds Big Futures LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to littlemindsbigfutures.com and the tutoring services we provide in Williamson County, Tennessee.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us — when you submit a form or contact us: your name, email, phone number, and details you choose to share about your child (such as age, grade, and learning goals) so we can tailor tutoring.
  • Automatic information — basic analytics (pages visited, device/browser type, general location) collected via Google Analytics to improve the site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and schedule a free consultation
  • To provide, personalize, and improve our tutoring services
  • To send you information you requested (such as the readiness checklist) and, with your consent, occasional updates or reminders
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website

SMS / text messaging

If you provide your phone number and consent (for example, by checking the consent box on our contact form), we may contact you by text message to schedule consultations, follow up on your inquiry, and send appointment reminders. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP; reply HELP for help. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any service.

We do not sell your information. Your mobile phone number and SMS consent information will never be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate our business — for example, our customer-relationship and messaging platform (HighLevel / LeadConnector) and website analytics (Google) — and only as needed to provide our services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & analytics

Our site uses cookies and Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as well.

Children's privacy

Our website and forms are intended for use by parents and guardians, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Any information about a child is provided to us by a parent or guardian to arrange tutoring, and is kept confidential and used only to deliver our services.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it. We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You may request to review, update, or delete your personal information, opt out of text messages (reply STOP), or unsubscribe from emails at any time. Contact us using the details below.
