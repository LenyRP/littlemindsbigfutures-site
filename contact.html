Common questions

Before you book

Do you offer in-home or online sessions? +

Both! In the Arrington, TN area I can come to you or you can come to me. For families further out, online sessions work beautifully for ages 5+.

How long are sessions and how often do we meet? +

Sessions are 45–60 minutes depending on age, typically once or twice a week. We'll figure out a rhythm that works during your consultation.

What does it cost? +

I'll walk you through pricing during the consultation so it's grounded in what your child actually needs — not a one-size-fits-all package. The consultation itself is always free.

Do you have to be Christian to work with you? +

Not at all. Faith shapes the heart behind my work, but I serve families of all backgrounds. We'll just be honest about what your family is looking for and meet there.
