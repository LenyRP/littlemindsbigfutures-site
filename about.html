Meet your tutor

Hi, I'm Leslie.

For over a decade I've had the privilege of walking alongside young learners as they discover the joy of reading — and the confidence that comes with it.

I started Little Minds, Big Futures because I believe every child deserves a tutor who sees them — not just their report card. My approach is rooted in structured literacy, hands-on learning, and the quiet faith that God has placed potential in every little mind that walks through my door.

I'm a mom, a lifelong learner, and someone who genuinely lights up when a four-year-old sounds out their first word or a seven-year-old reads their first chapter book cover to cover. That moment never gets old.

What I believe

The heart behind every lesson

Three convictions that shape how I show up for your family.

Every child matters.

No two learners are the same. I take time to understand how your child thinks, what excites them, and where they get stuck — then I meet them there.

Faith woven in, not bolted on.

For families who want a Christian foundation, that thread runs naturally through our time together — gentle, present, never preachy.

Progress over perfection.

The win isn't a flawless lesson — it's the moment your child reaches for a book on their own. We celebrate small steps, every week.
