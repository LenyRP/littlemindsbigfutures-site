Ages 3–4
Jumpstart Program

Kindergarten readiness starts here.

The years from 3 to 4 are the most important window in your child's academic journey — this is when curiosity is either nurtured or quietly squeezed. Jumpstart plants the seeds of a lifetime love of reading through play, laughter, and those priceless "I know this letter!" moments.

  • Letter recognition & phonics foundations
  • Listening & vocabulary growth
  • Read-aloud comprehension
  • Fine motor & pre-writing skills
  • Counting, shapes & number patterns
  • Confidence-building pace, every session

Leaping Program

Building the early literacy bridge — one confident leap at a time.

Whether your child is heading into kindergarten or just started first grade, this is the program that makes the difference. We turn the big leap into a series of small, exciting wins — each one a moment your child can feel.

  • Phonics & early decoding skills
  • Sight words & reading fluency
  • Beginning handwriting
  • Number sense & math foundations
  • Story sequencing & comprehension
  • Hands-on, game-based learning

Soaring Into Success

  • Reading fluency & stamina
  • Writing structure & expression

Getting started

Three simple steps

No complicated intake process. Just a conversation, a plan, and consistent progress.

1

Free 15-minute conversation

We talk about your child — strengths, challenges, goals. No commitment, no paperwork, no pressure. If we're a good fit, we figure out the next step together.

2

Assessment & personalized plan

A focused academic assessment to understand exactly where your child is. Then a clear, specific plan — not a one-size-fits-all curriculum, but a program built around your child.

3

Weekly sessions & real updates

Consistent sessions, measurable progress, and honest parent updates — so you always know where your child is and where they're headed. You're never left in the dark.
