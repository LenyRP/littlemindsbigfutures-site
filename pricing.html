Good to know

Pricing questions

Anything else on your mind? Just ask Leslie.

Is the consultation required before tutoring?+

The one-hour consultation is where we assess your child, set goals, and build a personalized plan — so it's the recommended starting point. You can also book a free 15-minute conversation first to be sure we're a good fit before anything else.

How do the tutoring packages save money?+

Booking sessions in a bundle earns a built-in discount — 5% on 6 sessions, 7% on 12 sessions, and 10% on 18 sessions — versus paying session by session. Packages also keep your child consistent, which is exactly where real progress happens.

How does the refer-a-friend discount work?+

When a family you refer starts tutoring, you receive 20% off your next session or package. It's our way of saying thank you for helping another child in Williamson County thrive.

Are in-home and online sessions the same price?+

Yes. In-home sessions across Williamson County — Arrington, Brentwood, Franklin, Nolensville, College Grove, and Chapel Hill — and online sessions are billed at the same rates. We'll pick whatever works best for your family and your child's age.
